The Great Auction

The story unfolds in a minimalist, very artsy space. After the avant-garde couple who lives in the house leaves for a weekend getaway, the faith of the precious art in the house is left to the notorious cat and the dog of the house. With the help of the butler, a great auction takes place. The real question is, what is the price of this precious avant-garde art?
